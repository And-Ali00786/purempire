to a hydrogenation process in which liquid unsaturated fat is turned

of trans fat in the oil which has an adverse effect. In the process of
extraction of vegetable oils from plants, high heat, pressure, and
chemical solvents are used. Thus the oils oxidize and become rancid,
as well as lose many or all of their properties. This renders them
toxic.
